Dr. T. Heath Ogden
Professor & Chair · Department of Biology
Utah Valley University · Orem, UT

Dr. Ogden is a Professor and Chair of the Biology Department at Utah Valley University. His research sits at the intersection of insect evolution, phylogenomics, and biology education. He uses molecular and morphological data to develop phylogenetic hypotheses for basal pterygotes and Ephemeroptera, and then leverages these trees to investigate evolutionary processes — including the origin of flight, evolution of mayfly morphology, and the forces shaping genome diversity.

He also carries out research on bioinformatics and sequence analysis, focusing on alignment accuracy, indel models, and phylogenetic inference methodology. A third research thread focuses on pedagogy: flipped classrooms, online vs. face-to-face instruction, and improving evolution acceptance among students.

Dr. Ogden received his BS in Zoology from BYU (1996), his MS from Universidad de Concepción, Chile (2000), and his PhD from BYU (2004), followed by a postdoctoral fellowship at Arizona State University. He joined UVU in 2009 and was named Chair of the Biology Department in 2023.
