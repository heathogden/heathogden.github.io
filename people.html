Principal Investigator

🧑‍🔬
Dr. Heath Ogden
Associate Professor of Biology · Utah Valley University

Dr. Ogden's research focuses on phylogenetic insect research, bioinformatics, and pedagogy. He investigates higher-level basal pterygote and ephemeropteran relationships using molecular and morphological data, and uses these phylogenies to evaluate evolutionary processes. He is also passionate about biology education research and evolution acceptance in students.
